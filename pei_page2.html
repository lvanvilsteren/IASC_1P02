My time in PEI

During my CWY trip, I first went to Prince Edward Island for 3 months. While I was there, I lived in a house family, with a single mother and two kids. Their names were Jennifer (mother), Olivia (daughter) and Ethan (son). My counterparts name was Praise, and she sung like an angel. While I was there I spent most of the time volunteering at Habit for Humanity, by working in their Restore, and by helping build at the site they happened to just start. We also as a group did lots of volunteer opportunities during our time there, which was nice, since it got us to learn more about the community and give back where we could. During my time spent with the family, we got to go to basketball games, out to eat, and explore the province a little more as well. They became my second family and the time I spent there will always be in my heart.
